المسؤولية الاجتماعية

The Saudi Authority for Industrial Estates and Technology Zones "MODON" is aware of the importance of social responsibility as one of the pillars of sustainable development in the modern era, and in this context it does not exercise its developmental role in the local economy in isolation from its societal role, as it works in conjunction with the relevant government and official agencies. According to long-term strategic goals, sub-goals fall under them: They are updated annually to keep pace with developments that may occur to Saudi society. Community initiatives

The participation of "MODON" in supporting and empowering Saudi women

Modon participated as a golden sponsor in a ceremony honoring 500 craftswomen at the "Saudi Women Creativity Center" in Jazan, with a working paper in a discussion session on social responsibility and support for Saudi women.

Health initiatives

The establishment of the first "Cities" sports championship

Modon organized the Modon Sports Championship from 26 September to 31 October 2018 at Arkan Sports Center in the Second Industrial City in Riyadh. The championship had the participation of 25 companies and factories, and 40 teams of employees of companies and factories compete, including 12 teams. In cricket, 10 teams in football and 18 teams in basketball

Environmental initiatives

Supporting the community initiative "We deserve our environment"

"Modon" participated in supporting the community initiative by launching an awareness-raising media campaign in a number of its industrial cities that included: “The first and second industrial cities in Riyadh, the first industrial city in Jeddah, and the first and second industrial cities in Dammam,” with the aim of raising awareness among community members of the importance of preserving the environment, and introducing Consumers of all groups stressed the importance of using biodegradable plastic products, and at the end of the campaign, MODON won the honor of His Royal Highness Prince Faisal bin Bandar bin Abdulaziz, Governor of Riyadh, for its role in community service.

أقامت "مدن" بالشراكة مع "يونيليفر السعودية" ورشة عمل بعنوان "صفر نفايات صناعية" وذلك بحضور ممثلين عن أكثر من 30مصنعاً في المدينة الصناعية الأولى بجدة.

توقيع اتفاقية تدوير

وقعت "مدن"، اتفاقية شراكة ثلاثية الأطراف، مع كل من الجمعية الخيرية لرعاية الأيتام بمنطقة الرياض "إنسان"، وشركة تدوير البيئة الأهلية "تدوير"، وذلك ضمن مبادرة "بصمتنا" التي تأتي تحت شعار "اكفل يتيم بجهازك القديم". ويتم خلال هذه المبادرة قيام الموظفين في "مدن" في كافة المدن الصناعية التابعة لها بكل أنحاء المملكة، بجمع المخلفات الإلكترونية والكهربائية مثل (أجهزة الكومبيوتر، الاتصالات، الثلاجات، المكيفات، الأفران.. وغيرها) وتقوم شركة "تدوير" بتقديم الحلول البديلة والآمنة بيئياً وصحياً لنقلها والتخلص منها طبقاً لمعايير التدوير المتبعة محلياً ودولياً.
